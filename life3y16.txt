今日课程：
Great!Now I have to re-do it.
这下可好了！我的重做了。
Hold on, you might be able to recover it.
等一下，你也许可以把它给恢复呢。
用法讲解：
1.Great!
太好了！这下好了！（太糟糕了）
That's just great.
2.re-do(something)
重做...
3.hold on
稍等一下
4.recover(something)
恢复、重新得到。。。。。
recover a memory
recover a lost object
recover the cost of an investment
recover the use of a body part(legs,fingers,etc.)after an accident
