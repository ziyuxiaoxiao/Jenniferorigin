There's some kind of funky odor in here.
这里有股臭味儿。
Maybe fish or something has gone bad.
可能是鱼或者什么东西变质了吧。
用法讲解：
1.some kind of (something)
多在不确切的情况下使用，表示有一些......
What's this? Some kind of noodle?
I hear some kind of noise.
2.a funky smell
[俚语]尤指因腐烂、变质、出汗产生的臭味
There's a funky smell in here.
There's something funky in that closet.
closet n.壁橱、储藏室
