今日课程：
He's adorable!
他好可爱啊！
Thank you.
谢谢。
He really resembles your husband.He has your eyes.
他跟你老公长得真像，他的眼睛像你。
用法讲解：
1.（Something/Someone) is adorable!
某物/某人非常可爱
常用来形容小孩、小动物等。
2.（someone) resembles (someone)
...长得像...
You resemble your mother.
She resembles her father.
3.(someone) has (someone's) (feature)
表示...的某个部位长得像...
You have your mother's nose.
